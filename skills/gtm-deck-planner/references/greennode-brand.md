## Core Products

### 1. GreenNode Cloud (AI Cloud Infrastructure)
The primary infrastructure product. Compete against AWS, Azure, GCP — but with local compliance, Vietnamese language support, and VNG ecosystem integration.

**Key services:**
- **GPU Cloud** — High-performance GPU clusters for AI training and inference (NVIDIA H100, A100, etc.)
- **Compute & Storage** — vCompute (VMs), vStorage, bare metal
- **Networking** — CDN, API Gateway, VKS (Kubernetes), vWAF, Anti-DDoS
- **AI Stack** — Pre-built ML/AI pipeline services for analytics, forecasting, computer vision
- **Security** — SOC, IAM, MFA, RBAC, IDS/IPS, DLP, SSO
- **Monitoring & Operations** — 24/7 VMS (Video Management System), centralized camera management

**Key value propositions for Cloud:**
- PAYG (Pay-As-You-Go) — up to 30-40% IT cost reduction vs on-premises
- Flexible hybrid/cloud migration (from VMware, on-premises, hyperscale)
- Compliance and security by design (Data Law 2024, PDPL 2025, Vietnam banking regulations)
- GPU Cloud purpose-built for AI workloads — not bolted on
- Local support team with deep Vietnamese market expertise
- Seasonal/elastic scalability for peak demand

### 2. GreenNode IDP (Intelligent Document Processing)
AI-powered document digitization platform combining traditional OCR + Large Language Model (LLM) technology.

**Core capabilities:**
- **Document OCR & Extraction** — Extract structured data from any document (invoices, contracts, ID cards, financial reports, insurance claims, medical records)
- **Document Fraud Detection** — 5-layer fraud analysis: metadata analysis, PDF content analysis, physical fraud check, image manipulation detection, information cross-check
- **Image Fraud Detection** — Detect copy-move, deepfake, document modifications, portrait replacements
- **Shelf Scoring** — AI-powered retail shelf compliance scoring: input image → crop shelf → detect products → score compliance (>98% accuracy, <1.5 seconds)
- **Warehouse Management** — Document recognition for receiving/shipping slips, delivery receipts, invoices, purchase orders

**IDP Deployment Models:**
- SaaS (cloud-hosted, scalable)
- On-premises (for regulated industries like banking)
- Hybrid

**IDP Key Stats:**
- Process 200+ types of documents
- 99% accuracy for printed text, 95% for handwriting
- Processing time < 30 seconds per record
- Reading performance: up to 1,800 images/second
- Deployed at banks processing 150M+ documents/year

**Primary taglines:**
- "Beyond Cloud Infrastructure"
- "High Performance Cloud AI for [Industry]"

**Vocabulary to use:**
- "AI-native", "purpose-built", "enterprise-grade", "high-performance", "elastic scalability"
- "digital transformation", "process automation", "intelligent document processing"

## Visual Identity Guidelines (for Design QA)

**Colors:**
- Primary: Dark green (#006037 range) and black — used for full-bleed section dividers
- Accent: Bright green (#00C853 range) — used for key highlights, call-outs, icon fills
- Secondary: Dark navy/teal (#004D4D range) — for data tables, secondary blocks
- Text backgrounds: White or very light grey for content slides
- Text: White on dark, dark charcoal on light

**Typography patterns:**
- Section titles: Very large (80-120pt), bold, often multiline
- Key stat callouts: Oversized numbers (200-400pt) in white on dark green
- Body text: Clean sans-serif, 14-18pt for bullets

**Layout patterns:**
- Full-bleed dark green/black header → white content body (most section openers)
- Split layout: text left, image/diagram right (most content slides)
- Icon grid: 4-6 icons with short labels (feature/benefit slides)
- Two-column comparison: "Pain Points" (light bg) vs "GreenNode Solution" (dark green bg)
- Case study layout: Customer logo + challenge (left) | Solution + results (right)
- Agenda slide: Numbered items (01/02/03...) with circle-arrow (→) icons

**Logo usage:**
- Always "GREENNODE." with the distinctive styling
- Tagline "a member of VNG Group" in small text under logo
- Logo placement: bottom-left of every slide

**Recurring motifs:**
- Green diagonal arrow (↗ or ⤵) on section headers
- "ready → to explore" button/badge on transition slides
- Circular ring/sphere 3D graphic used for "cloud ecosystem" concept
- Robotic arm holding basket = retail/logistics industry visual
- Holographic/AR overlay visuals for technology demonstration slides
